Welcome To Wander with Adam

Avery is an easygoing traveler with a camera always hanging from their neck. They prefer slow, unhurried trips where they can wander through side streets, talk with locals, and discover places not usually marked on maps. Instead of strict itineraries, Avery follows curiosity—choosing cozy cafés over crowded attractions and scenic backroads over highways. Their travel style is relaxed, thoughtful, and all about finding small moments worth remembering.

Destination Guides

Yellowstone National Park offers stunning geysers, colorful hot springs, and amazing wildlife all in one place. From Old Faithful to the Grand Prismatic Spring, every stop feels unforgettable. With scenic drives, peaceful trails, and wide-open views, Yellowstone is the perfect destination for anyone wanting adventure and natural beauty.
